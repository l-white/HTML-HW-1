Favorite Hobby

    I will admit it: I am a certified bibliophile. I love to read. Alice Sebold once said that she reads the way some people overeat. After reading a book, her head hurts, and she thinks, oh, I shouldn't have read that so fast! I can relate. My love for reading started with bedtime stories and story hour at the Barrington Area Library, and continued throughout college. Was majoring in English simply a way to read more? Perhaps.

Favorite Pastime

    If I said stamp collecting, would you believe me? Well, it's not stamp collecting. When I think of pastimes, I think family. I think one of my favorite pastimes is ice skating on the frozen lake behind my friend Megan's house. When I was young, I didn't mind the winter or the cold, especially if it meant ice skating, followed by hot chocolate.

Coffee with Friends

My favorite places to meet a friend for coffee in Milwaukee is Colectivo. When the weather is decent, it's a wonderful experience to sit outside, people watch, and connect with a good friend.

Bike Riding

Riding a bike downhill makes me feel incredibly carefree and youthful, so much so that I don't mind the occasional hill, which typically isn't a problem in the Midwest.

Photography

I became interested in photography in college and worked semi-professionally for a wedding photographer. Since then, landscape photography has become a wonderful source of relaxation.
